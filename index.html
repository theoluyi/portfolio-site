Hi there! My name is Theo Carney.
您好！我的名字叫陆毅。

I’ve always wanted to be a writer but I didn’t always want to write code.
How did the switch happen? I found myself in a world where digital tools
are reshaping everything, including our minds. As a neuroscience major,
Chinese speaker, music snob, and guy who cries at bad movies on airplanes,
I think a lot about how small changes in our sensory experience (input) can
have a huge impact on our subjective experience and behavior (output).


My ikigai is to find truth, beauty, and connection in life while helping
others to do the same, and to avoid causing harm in the process.
It’s corny but I think it’s worthy and necessary in today’s information
economy. Rather than simply critique systems and companies that
already exist, I want to work with other brilliant people to create a
better future through humane technology.

Experienced w/ JavaScript, React, Redux, Ruby, Rails, git, HTML/CSS. Familiar w/ PostgreSQL and Heroku.
